FAQ

  • 1. How do you set a goal amount of calorie intake?

    • You go to settings, general, goals, and then input calorie goal amount.

  • 2. Does the application track activity when in use?

    • Yes. You can change this setting to stop the app from tracking movement when in use/in the background. Keep in mind your total steps are used to calculate the total calories burned during daily activity. Turning off this feature excludes potentially burned calories.
